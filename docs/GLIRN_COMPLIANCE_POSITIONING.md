# GLIRN Compliance Positioning

## Identity

Public brand: David Sanson

Business name: Global Legal Intelligence & Recruitment Network

Legal and tax owner: Gareth Price

Positioning: Human-led, technology-enhanced, Gareth-approved.

## Compliance Position

GLIRN is designed as a compliance-first legal recruitment intelligence system.

It is not designed to automate recruitment decisions, scrape candidate data, or conduct outbound activity without human approval.

## Core Compliance Principles

- Human approval remains mandatory.
- Candidate consent controls are central.
- Client terms status must be recorded before client-facing candidate detail sharing.
- Every sensitive action should be audit logged.
- Data minimisation should guide candidate records.
- Legal market intelligence should avoid exposing candidate personal data unless consent is active.
- No autonomous outreach.
- No autonomous candidate introduction.
- No autonomous client engagement.
- No autonomous fee proposal.

## Human-In-The-Loop Controls

Gareth Price approval is required before:

- Candidate outreach.
- Candidate profile activation.
- Candidate introduction.
- Candidate detail sharing.
- Client outreach.
- Client engagement.
- Fee proposal.
- Report generation.
- Placement action.
- Integration activation.

## Candidate Consent Controls

GLIRN should block or flag activity where:

- Candidate consent is missing.
- Candidate consent is expired.
- Candidate-specific intelligence is requested without active consent.
- Candidate details would be shared without approval.

## Client Terms Controls

GLIRN should block or flag activity where:

- Client terms status is missing.
- Fee discussion is requested without terms readiness.
- Candidate details would be shared with a client before terms status is recorded.

## Data and Integration Controls

GLIRN does not currently enable:

- Scraping.
- Live internet fetching.
- External integrations.
- Candidate data ingestion.
- Autonomous outbound communications.
- Automated recruitment decisions.

Future integrations should remain subject to:

- Integration governance.
- Source readiness review.
- Lawful basis review.
- Candidate consent controls.
- Client terms controls.
- Gareth approval.
- Audit logging.

## Client-Facing Compliance Message

"GLIRN is human-led and compliance-first. It uses technology to structure intelligence, prioritise opportunities, and support recruitment judgement, but candidate introductions, client engagement, fee proposals, and candidate detail sharing remain subject to human approval and consent controls."

## Candidate-Facing Compliance Message

"GLIRN is designed to preserve candidate control. Candidate information should not be introduced or shared with clients without active consent and human approval."

## Boundaries

GLIRN does not provide legal advice, tax advice, financial advice, medical advice, or regulated recruitment automation.

Compliance readiness scores are internal control signals. They are not legal opinions.
