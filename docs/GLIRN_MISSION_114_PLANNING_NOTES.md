# Mission 114 Planning Notes

Status: Superseded by Gareth's approved Mission 114 Opportunity Intelligence Engine specification.

## Proposed Title

GLIRN Knowledge Governance and Controlled Change Framework

## Proposed Objective

Introduce a governed process for converting approved Mission 113 insights and Mission 113A evidence into traceable, manually implemented knowledge or policy change proposals.

## Proposed Scope

- Create versioned change proposals linked to approved learning insights and external evidence.
- Record the current rule, proposed rule, rationale, evidence references, expected effect, and rollback plan.
- Require impact assessment across Missions 105-113A safeguards.
- Require Gareth approval before a proposal can be marked ready for manual implementation.
- Require separate post-implementation verification and Gareth sign-off.
- Preserve an immutable audit history of proposal, approval, implementation, verification, and rollback status.

## Proposed Controls

- No automatic policy, threshold, prompt, workflow, or knowledge-base modification.
- No legal advice or automatic interpretation of regulatory obligations.
- No autonomous acceptance, decline, outreach, referral, payment, delivery, search, or external commitment.
- No external source retrieval or contact.
- No candidate-sensitive content duplicated in change records or audit events.
- Gareth remains the final decision-maker at approval and verification stages.

## Proposed Tests

- Unapproved insights cannot create an implementation-ready change.
- Unapproved external intelligence cannot update the knowledge base.
- Every proposal links to its supporting evidence and approval records.
- Safeguard impact assessment is mandatory.
- Implementation and verification are separate manual states.
- Rejected or rolled-back proposals cannot become active.
- Existing Missions 105-113A safeguards remain intact.
- Full regression suite remains green.

## Approval Boundary

These earlier planning notes are retained for audit history. They do not describe the approved Mission 114 scope, which is the Opportunity Intelligence Engine documented in the runbook.
