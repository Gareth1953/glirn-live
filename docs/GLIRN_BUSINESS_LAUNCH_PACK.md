# GLIRN Business Launch Pack

## Identity

Public brand: David Sanson

Business name: Global Legal Intelligence & Recruitment Network

Legal and tax owner: Gareth Price

Positioning: Human-led, technology-enhanced, Gareth-approved.

## Core Proposition

Global Legal Intelligence & Recruitment Network helps legal employers identify high-value hiring needs, understand market demand, and evaluate senior legal recruitment opportunities before any outreach or introduction occurs.

The service combines legal market intelligence with recruitment execution. Intelligence opens the client conversation; recruitment placements create the primary commercial value.

## Service Offering

- Legal market intelligence summaries.
- Practice area demand analysis.
- Jurisdiction demand analysis.
- Salary and fee opportunity intelligence.
- Senior legal candidate mapping.
- Client hiring opportunity prioritisation.
- Partner, General Counsel, Chief Legal Officer, Legal Director, and Senior Associate search support.
- Human-approved candidate-to-client match review.
- Compliance-first readiness checks before any candidate or client action.

## Fee Model

Primary revenue:

- Contingency placement fee.
- Retained search fee.
- Executive search fee.

Secondary revenue:

- Legal intelligence report fee.
- Subscription intelligence fee.

Indicative fee basis:

- Contingency placement: percentage of first-year compensation.
- Executive search: higher percentage due to seniority and complexity.
- Retained search: staged retainer against expected placement value.
- Intelligence reports: fixed fee or recurring subscription where appropriate.

All fee proposals require Gareth Price approval.

## Compliance Positioning

GLIRN is compliance-first and human-controlled.

- No candidate introduction without active consent.
- No candidate details shared without active consent and Gareth approval.
- No client engagement without recorded client terms status.
- No fee proposal without Gareth approval.
- No outbound action without Gareth approval.
- No scraping.
- No live external data ingestion unless approved in a future governance process.
- No automated recruitment decision-making.

## Human-In-The-Loop Controls

Gareth Price approval is required for:

- Candidate outreach.
- Candidate profile activation.
- Candidate introduction.
- Client outreach.
- Client engagement.
- Candidate detail sharing.
- Fee proposal.
- Intelligence report generation.
- Placement action.
- Integration activation.

## Client Hook

The client hook is legal intelligence plus recruitment.

GLIRN should first help a client understand:

- Which practice areas are heating up.
- Which jurisdictions are showing demand.
- Where salary pressure is rising.
- Which roles are commercially important.
- Which legal hiring gaps may create operational risk.

Recruitment then follows only where there is a clear, approved, compliant hiring opportunity.

## Launch Principle

GLIRN should launch narrowly:

- One client type.
- One practice area.
- One jurisdiction.
- One human-approved workflow.
- No autonomous outreach.
- No live integrations.
- No candidate data sharing without consent.
