# GLIRN Candidate Value Proposition

## Identity

Public brand: David Sanson

Business name: Global Legal Intelligence & Recruitment Network

Legal and tax owner: Gareth Price

Positioning: Human-led, technology-enhanced, Gareth-approved.

## Candidate Problem

Senior legal candidates need discretion, relevance, and control.

They do not want:

- Their details shared without consent.
- Generic recruiter outreach.
- Poorly matched roles.
- Unclear client interest.
- Pressure to engage with unsuitable opportunities.
- Automated decisions about their career.

## Candidate Promise

GLIRN is designed to identify high-quality legal opportunities while preserving human approval, consent, and candidate control.

Candidate information should only be used where consent is active and the opportunity is relevant.

## Candidate Benefits

- Better-matched opportunities.
- Less irrelevant outreach.
- Human-reviewed role suitability.
- Clear consent boundaries.
- No candidate introduction without approval.
- No client-facing candidate detail sharing without consent.
- Stronger fit between practice area, seniority, jurisdiction, and role value.

## Candidate Trust Position

GLIRN should communicate that it is:

- Consent-aware.
- Human-controlled.
- Not a scraping engine.
- Not an automated decision-maker.
- Not a mass outreach tool.
- Designed for senior legal recruitment where discretion matters.

## Candidate Controls

GLIRN requires:

- Active consent before candidate introduction.
- Active consent before candidate details are shared.
- Gareth Price approval before candidate outreach.
- Gareth Price approval before candidate profile activation.
- Audit logging for relevant approval decisions.

## Candidate-Facing Language

"Your profile is treated as a controlled, consent-based record. No introduction or client-facing candidate detail sharing should happen without active consent and human approval."

## Boundaries

GLIRN does not:

- Guarantee roles.
- Guarantee placement.
- Provide career advice as a regulated service.
- Share personal data without consent.
- Automate candidate acceptance or rejection decisions.
