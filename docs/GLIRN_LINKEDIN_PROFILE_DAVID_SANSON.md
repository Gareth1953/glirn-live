## About

I work at the intersection of legal market intelligence and senior legal recruitment.

Global Legal Intelligence & Recruitment Network helps identify high-value legal hiring opportunities across law firms, in-house legal teams, private equity-backed companies, infrastructure, energy, and legal technology.

The focus is not volume recruitment. It is structured, intelligence-led judgement:

- Which legal roles matter most.
- Which practice areas are showing demand.
- Which jurisdictions are becoming more active.
- Which senior candidate opportunities deserve human review.
- Which client hiring needs may justify a search.

GLIRN is human-led, technology-enhanced, and approval-controlled. Candidate introductions, client engagement, and fee proposals remain subject to human review, consent awareness, and compliance-first controls.
