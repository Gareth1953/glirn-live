## 8. V1 Completion Decision

Decision: V1 Complete With Conditions

Justification:

GLIRN V1 has achieved the original operating design. It can operate internally from opportunity detection to final Gareth approval package without Gareth manually driving each preparation step.

The conditions are:

- GLIRN must remain restricted from autonomous external activity.
- Gareth must approve any real-world action.
- Billing, payment, and money movement remain human-controlled.
- Broader launch should wait until the first controlled revenue validation has been completed.
