# GLIRN Client Value Proposition

## Identity

Public brand: David Sanson

Business name: Global Legal Intelligence & Recruitment Network

Legal and tax owner: Gareth Price

Positioning: Human-led, technology-enhanced, Gareth-approved.

## Client Problem

Legal employers often know they need stronger legal capability, but they may not know:

- Which role to prioritise.
- Which practice area is most commercially urgent.
- Whether the market is moving against them.
- What level of candidate is realistic.
- What fee exposure to expect.
- Whether a search is worth starting now.

## Client Promise

GLIRN helps clients move from vague hiring pressure to a clear, ranked, human-reviewed legal recruitment opportunity.

It does this by combining legal market intelligence, fee visibility, role prioritisation, and human-approved recruitment controls.

## What Clients Receive

- A clear view of legal hiring demand.
- Practice area and jurisdiction signals.
- Salary and fee intelligence.
- Prioritised role recommendations.
- Candidate-market insight where consent allows.
- Human-reviewed search readiness.
- Compliance-first candidate introduction controls.

## Why Clients Pay

Clients pay because weak legal hiring decisions are expensive.

GLIRN helps reduce:

- Time wasted on unclear hiring briefs.
- Missed senior legal hires.
- Poorly timed searches.
- Fee uncertainty.
- Compliance and consent risk.
- Unstructured recruitment processes.

## Target Client Profiles

- Mid-sized law firms needing senior lateral hires.
- Boutique firms seeking specialist capability.
- In-house legal teams expanding under pressure.
- Private equity-backed companies professionalising legal functions.
- Infrastructure and energy companies with complex legal needs.
- Legal tech companies needing commercial legal leadership.

## Client Hook

The first client conversation should not be a generic recruitment pitch.

The hook is:

"Here is what the legal talent market appears to be signalling in your practice area or jurisdiction, and here is the role GLIRN would prioritise first."

## Trust Position

GLIRN is not positioned as autonomous AI recruitment.

It is positioned as:

- Human-led.
- Technology-enhanced.
- Consent-aware.
- Audit-friendly.
- Gareth-approved.

## Boundaries

GLIRN does not:

- Scrape candidate data.
- Send autonomous outreach.
- Share candidate details without consent.
- Make automated recruitment decisions.
- Replace human judgement.
- Provide legal advice to clients.
