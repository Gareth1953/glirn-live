## What GLIRN Is

GLIRN is a legal intelligence and recruitment network focused on identifying high-value legal hiring opportunities and senior legal candidate matches.

It combines market intelligence, recruitment judgement, compliance controls, and human approval.

## What GLIRN Does

- Identifies legal hiring demand.
- Ranks senior legal recruitment opportunities.
- Assesses practice area and jurisdiction signals.
- Prioritises target client firms and legal departments.
- Prioritises senior legal candidates.
- Estimates placement and retained search fee potential.
- Reviews candidate-to-client match quality.
- Applies consent, client terms, audit, and approval controls.

Secondary revenue:

## Client Hook

Legal intelligence plus recruitment.

GLIRN should help clients understand the market before starting a search:

- Which practice area is heating up.
- Which jurisdiction is active.
- Which legal role should be prioritised.
- What fee exposure may be realistic.
- Whether a search is worth starting.

## Compliance Position

GLIRN is compliance-first and human-controlled.

- No autonomous outreach.
- No candidate introduction without consent.
- No candidate detail sharing without consent and approval.
- No client engagement without approval.
- No fee proposal without approval.
- No live integrations unless governed and approved.

## Core Message

GLIRN is not volume recruitment and not autonomous AI recruitment.

It is a human-led, technology-enhanced legal intelligence and recruitment system designed to identify better opportunities, reduce poor-fit activity, and keep candidate and client actions under human control.
