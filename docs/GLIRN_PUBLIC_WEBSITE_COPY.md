## Client Hook

Legal intelligence first. Recruitment second.

GLIRN helps clients understand legal market pressure before deciding whether to start a search. The aim is to identify the role, practice area, jurisdiction, and seniority level most likely to create business value.

## Trust Position

GLIRN is not an autonomous recruitment bot.

It is:

- Human-led.
- Technology-enhanced.
- Consent-aware.
- Approval-controlled.
- Audit-friendly.

## Compliance Message

Candidate introductions and candidate detail sharing require active consent and human approval. Client engagement and fee proposals require human approval. Future integrations remain governed, controlled, and inactive unless approved.
