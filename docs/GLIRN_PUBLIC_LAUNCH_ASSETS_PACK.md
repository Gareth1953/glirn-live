Senior legal hiring is expensive, competitive, and often unclear.

- Which practice areas show strongest demand?
- How difficult may the hire be?
- What type of candidate profile is realistic?
- Should the client start a search, monitor the market, or defer?

GLIRN is built around a simple principle:

Better hiring intelligence leads to better recruitment decisions.
