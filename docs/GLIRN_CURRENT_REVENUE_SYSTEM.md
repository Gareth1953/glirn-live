# GLIRN Current Revenue System

## Purpose

The current GLIRN revenue system shows how legal intelligence and recruitment opportunities may convert into fees while keeping all commercial action human-approved.

It is a revenue command and preparation layer, not an autonomous sales, outreach, delivery, contracting, or invoicing system.

Current test status: 226 passed.

## Revenue Modules

### Commercial Revenue Engine

Tracks the estimated commercial pipeline.

Revenue types:

- Contingency placement fee.
- Retained search fee.
- Executive search fee.
- Intelligence report fee.
- Subscription intelligence fee.

Controls:

- No invoice readiness unless client terms are recorded.
- No fee proposal without Gareth approval.
- No candidate submission without active consent.
- Every commercial action must be audit logged.

### Automated Intelligence Review Engine

Generates a draft GLIRN Senior Legal Hiring Intelligence Review.

Revenue role:

- Creates the intelligence hook for a potential client conversation.
- Frames hiring pressure, practice area demand, jurisdiction demand, and role priority.
- Helps move from market insight to possible search mandate.

Control:

- Draft only until Gareth approval.

### Client Deliverable Factory

Generates draft client-facing documents.

Revenue role:

- Converts internal intelligence into commercial collateral.
- Supports search mandate proposals, executive search proposals, fee proposals, shortlist reports, market reports, and meeting briefs.

Control:

- No deliverable becomes client-ready without Gareth approval.

### Approval-to-Action Workflow

Controls draft movement into human-use readiness.

Revenue role:

- Separates generated drafts from approved material.
- Provides a queue of pending, approved, rejected, and monitored items.

Control:

- Approved means ready for human-controlled use, not automatic delivery.

### Revenue Command Centre

Provides the executive revenue cockpit.

Revenue role:

- Shows total revenue pipeline.
- Shows estimated placement fee pipeline.
- Shows estimated intelligence review revenue.
- Identifies highest fee opportunity.
- Identifies fastest revenue opportunity.
- Calculates revenue readiness score.
- Ranks top revenue opportunities and quick wins.
- Shows the funnel from opportunity to invoice ready.

Control:

- Read-only.
- No revenue action is executed.

## Current Revenue Workflow

1. Opportunity

GLIRN identifies and ranks legal recruitment opportunities.

2. Intelligence Review

GLIRN generates a draft intelligence review using internal platform data.

3. Search Mandate

GLIRN generates a draft search mandate proposal or related client deliverable.

4. Candidate Match

GLIRN ranks candidate-to-client matches subject to consent and terms gates.

5. Placement

GLIRN estimates placement fee potential and placement probability.

6. Invoice Ready

Invoice readiness requires client terms, active candidate consent where relevant, compliance readiness, and Gareth approval.

## Current Revenue Metrics

The Revenue Command Centre tracks:

- Total Revenue Pipeline.
- Estimated Placement Fee Pipeline.
- Estimated Intelligence Review Revenue.
- Approved Opportunities Count.
- Approved Deliverables Count.
- Highest Fee Opportunity.
- Fastest Revenue Opportunity.
- Revenue Readiness Score.
- Top Revenue Opportunities.
- Top Quick Win Opportunities.
- Highest Probability Revenue.

## Safety Rules

- No autonomous outreach.
- No autonomous client delivery.
- No autonomous fee proposals.
- No autonomous contracts.
- No autonomous invoicing.
- No external integrations.
- No scraping.
- No live data fetching.
- No automated recruitment decisions.
- No candidate personal data without active consent.
- No capital execution.
- Human approval remains mandatory.

## Remaining Gaps Before First Client

- Confirm first target client profile.
- Confirm first narrow service offer.
- Confirm human review checklist for generated intelligence reviews.
- Confirm human review checklist for generated deliverables.
- Confirm candidate consent process.
- Confirm client terms process.
- Confirm compliance review position.
- Confirm manual delivery process outside the system.
- Confirm fee model for first engagement.
- Confirm payment and invoicing process outside the system.

Create a first-client readiness gate that defines exactly what must be checked before Gareth uses any GLIRN-generated review or deliverable with a real prospect.
