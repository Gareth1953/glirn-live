## Product Title

AI Agent Safety Gate Starter Pack

## Subtitle

Practical approval rules, checklists, and test cases for safer AI automations in Zapier, Make, and n8n.

## Short Description

Stop AI automations from sending, spending, publishing, or acting without a safety check. This starter pack gives automation builders a plain-English ALLOW / REQUEST_APPROVAL / BLOCK policy, workflow guides, test cases, client reassurance language, and a pilot runbook for AI-generated customer email workflows.

## Long Description

AI automations are useful, but customer-facing actions need guardrails.

This pack helps no-code builders, AI workflow consultants, and small automation agencies add a simple safety step before an AI-generated customer email is sent.

Before the workflow acts, it asks:

- ALLOW
- REQUEST_APPROVAL
- BLOCK

The pack is designed for practical workflows in Zapier, Make, n8n, Airtable, Gmail, Slack, CRM tools, or similar automation stacks.
