## Long Description

AI automations are useful. Customer-facing AI actions still need guardrails.

If you build AI workflows for yourself or clients, sooner or later the same question appears:

```text
What stops the AI from sending the wrong thing?
```

The AI Agent Safety Gate Starter Pack helps no-code automation builders, AI workflow consultants and small automation agencies add a simple safety decision before an AI-generated customer email is sent or acted on.

Before the workflow acts, it asks:

- ALLOW
- REQUEST_APPROVAL
- BLOCK

The pack is designed for practical workflows in Zapier, Make, n8n, Airtable, Gmail, Slack, CRM tools and similar automation stacks.

It is deliberately narrow. It focuses on one common workflow risk:

```text
AI drafts a customer email, but the workflow needs a safety check before action.
```

Use it to explain and test when an AI workflow should continue, pause for human approval, or stop completely.

## FAQ

### Is this software?

No. This is a digital starter pack: policy, checklists, workflow guides, test cases, client reassurance copy and a pilot runbook.

### Does it include Zapier, Make or n8n templates?

It includes workflow placement guides and branch logic. It does not include prebuilt platform templates.

### Is this legal, medical or financial advice?

No. It is not legal advice, medical advice, regulated financial advice, tax advice, investment advice, security advice or compliance advice.

### Can I use it for client work?

Yes, the pack is written for automation builders and small agencies who need a clearer safety explanation for AI email workflows. Do not use it as legal/compliance documentation.

### Does this stop all AI mistakes?

No. It gives you a practical safety policy and test structure. You still need human review, sensible workflow design and fail-closed handling.

### What is the safest first use?

One AI-generated customer email workflow where REQUEST_APPROVAL stops the send and a human reviews manually.

### Can I use it for workflows that spend money or change vendors?

The pack treats spending money and vendor/tool changes as BLOCK in the V1 safety policy.

## Buyer Objections

### "Zapier already has approvals."

Correct. Zapier can pause for approval. This pack helps you decide when a workflow should allow, request approval or block before the approval step.

### "I can build this myself."

Yes. This pack saves you from starting with a blank page. It gives you ready-to-use rules, checklists, test cases and client explanation language.

### "This seems too simple."

That is intentional. The safest first version is narrow: one workflow, AI-generated customer email, three decisions.

### "I need enterprise governance."

This is not an enterprise governance product. It is a practical starter pack for no-code and small automation workflows.

### "I do not want to handle private data."

The pack explicitly excludes private-data-heavy workflows and treats customer-facing private data as a BLOCK case.
