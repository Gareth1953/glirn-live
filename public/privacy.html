Privacy Policy

GLIRN handles enquiries, client information, and candidate information carefully and confidentially.

Information submitted through the enquiry form is used to assess the enquiry and prepare a human-reviewed response where appropriate. A fixed receipt acknowledgement may be sent automatically. Substantive responses and paid work remain subject to human review.

Candidate-specific information is not used or shared without appropriate consent. GLIRN does not use website enquiries for automated outreach, automated candidate contact, automatic candidate sharing, scraping, or live data fetching.
